# 3D Stylized Image Generation Prompts

This document contains detailed prompts for generating character images in a **"High-End 3D Stylized"** style. The priority is to make the characters look like **tangible, volumetric 3D models** (similar to Pixar, Disney, or high-quality game cinematics) while ensuring they are **instantly recognizable**.

## Global Style Guide
> **Style:** **High-End 3D Animated Character.** Think Pixar, Disney, or Fortnite cinematic trailers.
> **Priority:** **Maximum 3D Depth & Volume.** The image must pop with 3D form. Use rim lighting, subsurface scattering (wax-like skin glow), and ambient occlusion.
> **Likeness:** **Caricatured Realism.** Exaggerate iconic features (e.g., Lincoln's height, Stalin's mustache, Churchill's jowls) to ensure the person is known immediately, even in this stylized form.
> **Lighting:** Studio lighting with strong rim lights to separate the character from the background.
